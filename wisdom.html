Questions

The five things parents always ask first.

Is Wisdom actually free?+

Yes. The core experience — all four kingdoms, AR breathing, meditations — is free to download and use. No credit card. No trial period. A subscription unlocks additional storybooks and the full educator curriculum.

What ages is it built for?+

Designed primarily for ages 4–8. Younger kids enjoy it with a parent; older kids (up to ~10) tell us they still use it independently for breathing and meditations.

Is it safe? Will it track my child?+

No ads, ever. No tracking. COPPA-certified, FERPA-compliant, GDPR-ready. Built with child psychologists, audited for accessibility, designed to be inclusive for kids with autism and ADHD.

How is this different from Calm Kids or Headspace?+

Those are meditation libraries with a child skin. Wisdom is a story-driven game where the meditation is the reward, not the chore. It's evidence-based SEL — closer to a classroom curriculum than a relaxation app.

Does it work for schools and districts?+

Yes. We work with schools across the US and EU. Includes the 14-week read-aloud SEL program, classroom-ready lesson plans, Schoolwork integration for iPad, and per-seat licensing. Request a demo →
